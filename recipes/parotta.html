PAROTTA

This is my all time favorite food

This is a hot and rough food. Also Bun Parotta is very popular mainly in Madurai (a city in tamilnadu)

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cup maida
  • 2 tbsp rava / semolina / suji (fine)
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp ghee / clarified butter
  • water (for kneading)
  • oil (for soaking & roasting)
